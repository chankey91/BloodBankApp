## üí° Best Practices

### **For Blood Banks:**

#### **Planning:**
1. **Schedule in Advance**
   - Create camps at least 1-2 weeks ahead
   - Gives donors time to plan

2. **Choose Accessible Locations**
   - Well-known venues
   - Easy to reach
   - Adequate parking/public transport

3. **Set Realistic Targets**
   - Based on location and capacity
   - Consider staff and equipment

4. **Provide Clear Information**
   - Detailed description
   - Accurate address
   - Working contact number

#### **Communication:**
1. **Use Notification Feature**
   - Always check "Notify nearby donors"
   - Reaches potential donors automatically

2. **Respond to Queries**
   - Monitor contact email/phone
   - Answer donor questions promptly

3. **Send Reminders**
   - 1 day before camp
   - Morning of camp

#### **On Camp Day:**
1. **Setup Early**
   - Arrive 1-2 hours before
   - Set up equipment and registration desk

2. **Welcome Donors**
   - Friendly staff
   - Clear signage
   - Efficient process

3. **Provide Refreshments**
   - Juice, biscuits, fruits
   - Water availability
   - Rest area

4. **Record Donations**
   - Use system to record each donation
   - Award points and badges
   - Issue certificates

### **For Donors:**

#### **Before Registration:**
1. **Check Eligibility**
   - At least 18 years old
   - Weight > 45 kg
   - Last donation > 56 days ago
   - No current health issues

2. **Review Camp Details**
   - Date and time
   - Location and directions
   - Contact person

#### **After Registration:**
1. **Mark Your Calendar**
   - Set reminder for camp day
   - Plan your schedule

2. **Prepare Yourself**
   - Eat iron-rich foods
   - Stay hydrated
   - Get good sleep

#### **On Camp Day:**
1. **Arrive on Time**
   - Not too early, not late
   - Avoid rush hours if possible

2. **Bring Required Items**
   - Valid ID proof
   - Previous donation card

3. **Be Patient**
   - May be some wait time
   - Process takes 30-45 mins total

4. **Follow Instructions**
   - Listen to medical staff
   - Report any discomfort
   - Rest as advised
